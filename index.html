Month 1 — June 15 · Welcome Home. Know Where You Are.
Week 1
Jun 15–21
Launch Week. Community opens. Welcome video live. CEO Self-Audit drops. Introduce yourself in the feed. 7-Day Enrollment Audit Challenge begins. Resource: CEO Self-Audit Workbook.
Week 2
Jun 22–28
First Live Webinar. Topic: Know Your Numbers Before You Build Anything — The CEO Dashboard. Live session + Q&A. Replay posted within 24 hours. Resource: CEO Weekly Dashboard Template.
Week 3
Jun 29–Jul 5
First Hot Seat. 3–4 founding members coached live. 15 minutes each. Submit your question in the Hot Seat Room before June 29th. Replay posted within 24 hours.
Week 4
Jul 6–14
Challenge + Close. 7-Day Enrollment Audit Challenge closes. CEO of the Month announced. Resource: Enrollment Pipeline Checklist. Month 1 closing post from Aliya.
Month 2 — July 15 · Build Your Team. Know Your Money.
Week 5
Jul 15–21
Month 2 Opens + July 18th Live Event. VIP founding members attend the Full Circle CEO Experience in Philadelphia. Resource: Staff Onboarding SOP Template. Community energy is HIGH this week.
Week 6
Jul 22–28
Second Live Webinar. Topic: Build a Team That Doesn't Need You in the Room. Staffing, onboarding, retention, internal leaders. Resource: Staff Evaluation Form.
Week 7
Jul 29–Aug 4
Second Hot Seat + Book Club Launch. Book Club announced for founding members + Inner Circle clients only. First book revealed. First discussion date set.
Week 8
Aug 5–14
Challenge + Close. 7-Day Team Audit Challenge. CEO of the Month announced. Resource: Finance Tracker / Cash Flow Template drops.
Month 3 — August 15 · Step Into the CEO Seat.
Week 9
Aug 15–21
Third Live Webinar. Topic: The CEO Transition — How to Stop Running Your Center and Start Leading It. 60 minutes. Resource: Visionary CEO Transition Roadmap.
Week 10
Aug 22–28
Third Hot Seat. 75 minutes — bigger, deeper, more vulnerable. Focus: CEO identity + decision-making. Second Book Club discussion this month.
Week 11
Aug 29–Sep 7
Live 90-Day Planning Workshop. Founding members only. Aliya walks you through building your Q4 plan live — she fills hers in alongside you. Resource: 90-Day Planning Template.
Week 12
Sep 8–14
Celebration + What's Next. 90-day wins shared. Aliya celebrates every founding member by name. Q4 preview revealed. Personal DM from Aliya to every founding member. Resource: Q4 CEO Growth Plan Template.
